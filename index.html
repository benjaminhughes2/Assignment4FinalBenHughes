My Angle

After being introduced to the Adobe Suite by my older brother when I was about 12 years old, I quickly became obsessed. The passion, which started with me photoshopping photographs I had taken, rapidly turned to me constantly creating interfaces with these applications. As a UX Student at Wilfred Laurier University, I am able to apply what I have learned over the years and provide a unique optic on user experience design.

Through my work, I apply a strong focus on creating for users with disabilities. This applies to both physical and mental disabilities that much of our population faces.
